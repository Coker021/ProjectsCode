    Which smartphone has the best camera?

    Determining the smartphone with the best camera can be subjective and dependent on various factors such as photography style, personal preferences, and budget. However, some of the popular smartphones that are known for their cameras are:
    Apple iPhone: The latest iPhone models, such as the iPhone 14 Pro Max, iPhone 14 Pro, and iPhone 14, have impressive camera systems that offer high-quality photos and videos.
    Samsung Galaxy: Samsung smartphones such as the Galaxy S21 Ultra, Galaxy S21+, and Galaxy S21 have some of the best camera systems in the Android market, with features like 8K video recording and a 108-megapixel main camera.
    Google Pixel: The Google Pixel series has a reputation for having one of the best smartphone cameras, with the latest Pixel 7 and Pixel 7 Pro featuring a large image sensor and AI-powered image processing technology.
    Huawei Mate: Huawei's Mate 40 Pro and P40 Pro have innovative camera systems that offer a range of features, such as 50x digital zoom, improved low-light performance, and improved image stabilisation.
    OPPO Find X5 Pro: Powered by Qualcomm's flagship Snapdragon 8 Gen 1 SoC, the triple camera setup comprises a 50-Megapixel primary shooter, a separate ultra-wide camera, and a 13-Megapixel telephoto lens with 2x optical zoom.
    It's essential to keep in mind that the camera quality also depends on factors such as lighting, the environment, and the user's skill level. Hence, it's best to research and read reviews before making a purchase.
